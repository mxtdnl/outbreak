Situation Briefing

A novel pathogen has been confirmed in Meridian City (population 500,000). You are the Emergency Public Health Director. Over 12 simulated weeks you will make weekly resource and policy decisions within a hard weekly budget. All decisions bind.

The population is split into a general group and a smaller clinically vulnerable group with far higher fatality risk — how you protect each shapes both deaths and public life. Events will interrupt you; some can be blunted by early investment. Choose your scenario and difficulty below.
